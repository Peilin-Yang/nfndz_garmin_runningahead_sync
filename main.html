[NFNDZ]Garmin Runningahead Sync

Please Read Carefully Before Use This Toolkit!!!

  • Use this toolkit at your own risk. The author takes no responsibility of the possible problems it brings.
  • Please upgrade Chrome to the latest version.
  • Please make sure that your Garmin connect and Runningahead are logged in within the same web browser windows.
  • The toolkit will download all the activities logs to your local disk. The target directory will be nfndz_garmin_logs in your "Downloads" directory. Please make sure there are enough space.
  • You can opt to sync the logs to Runningahead. Also, you can choose to remove the log files from your local disk once there are successfully synced.
